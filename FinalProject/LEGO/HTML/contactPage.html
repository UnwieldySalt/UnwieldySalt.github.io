Important Hazards.
There are many pieces in a Lego Set that may be cause of harm for children under the age of 10.

Potential hazards could be a result from:
1. Ingestion of small lego pieces
2. Injuries due to pointy lego pieces
3. Injuries resulting from electrical components in a set.

Please look out for for warning labels such as this one to make to assess the safety of a specific set for your child.
